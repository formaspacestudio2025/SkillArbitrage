Why This Niche is Emerging

Market Gaps & Pain Points

  • Field teams see digital twin discussions as “vaporware” — ideas exist but practical workflows are missing.
  • BIM software often fails to elegantly sync with live site data.
  • No standard predictive frameworks for schedule risk, cost variability, or progress ingestion.
  • Integration complexity across BIM, schedule, cost, and IoT data.
